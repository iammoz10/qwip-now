How it works

Three steps.
Done in under a minute.

01
Name it. Set a target.

Type what it's for. Add an optional target amount. Your round is live and ready to share. Takes about thirty seconds, and that's if you type slowly.

~30 seconds
02
Share the link or QR.

You get a short link and a QR code instantly. WhatsApp the group. Drop it in Slack. Hold up the QR at the match. However works for your crowd.

Instant
03
One tap and they're in.

People open the link, pick an amount, pay with Apple or Google Pay. Face ID confirms. No account, no sign-up, no card details to type. That really is it.
